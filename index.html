FAQS

Is hiring a digital marketing agency worth it?
Yes, hiring digital marketing services is a worthwhile investment because it is highly productive, increasingly cost-effective, and may assist you in gaining an unbiased perspective. Hiring a digital advertising firm also grants you access to their marketing resources, established and proven marketing strategies, and cutting-edge technology.

What type of businesses do you work with?
Keenly Digital works with both large and small businesses. Whether you're a small business trying to reach your local audience or a major, global brand looking to launch a new product in multiple markets, we have digital marketing specialists that have the knowledge and passion required to make your marketing campaign a success.

Which services do you offer?
Keenly Digital offers various services, including social media marketing, content writing, PPC campaigning, SEO optimization, web development and design, graphic designing, link building, content generation, logo designing, search engine marketing, email outreach, and several others.

Is Keenly Digital capable of handling the complete digital side of my business?
Yes, we can digitally handle every aspect of your brand. We can create a good website and the relevant content for it, as well as maintain it on a regular basis. We can also create a strong social media presence across all platforms, employing both textual and graphic content. We also have experience with digital advertising and marketing. We can grow an audience from zero to millions. Keenly Digital can be the best partner for a brand if it allows us to promote it in the digital sphere.
What is the purpose of a digital marketing agency?
The purpose and aim of a digital marketing agency is to provide a wide range of digital marketing services under one roof. The ultimate goal is to increase the website traffic, conversion rates, and sales of a business.

Why are digital marketing services important for my business?
One of the main reasons digital marketing services have grown in importance over traditional marketing techniques is that the internet allows businesses to communicate with target audiences in real time. Furthermore, as compared to traditional advertising techniques, using digital marketing services is relatively affordable and has an unlimited reach.
Is digital marketing cost-effective?
Yes, digital marketing is one of the most affordable and cost-effective forms of business marketing. With digital marketing, it is easier for you to effectively measure the results of your marketing campaigns when compared to other traditional types of marketing, allowing you to make informed decisions regarding the value it provides.
How often will I receive reports on my account?
In addition to daily, as-needed communication, we will schedule a weekly meeting with you and your key stakeholders. This enables us to set expectations for weekly tasks, offer incremental reports, and make timely decisions.
